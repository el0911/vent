How we work

Vent is a simple website with one landing page that allows users to let -out their frustrations and sadness at teh click f a button that redirects them to a secure one-on-one WhatsApp chat with a "listener". A listener is a person who receives the anonymous user in a secure chat and listens to what the user has to say without giving advice or opinion except when requested by the user.


Our aim

Our sole aim is to create a safe space for people to share their frustrations and problems without fear of judgement, cyber -bullying or condemnation. We beleive that people want a listening party that can lend them an ear to their problemswithout necessarily telling them what to do. Even the most basic things such as being stuck in traffic can disrupt a persons mood for the day and can affect productivity. We want to give people the chance to talk about what is hurting them ans have them rest assured that they will receive zero backlash from self-disclosure.
